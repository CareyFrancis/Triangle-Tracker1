TRIANGLE TRACKER.

There are different types of triangles that exist. We'll be looking at 3 types of triangles in this app.

  • Equilateral Triangle
    • - This type of triangle is one that has all sides and angles equal.All angles are also equal to 60 degrees.
  • Isosceles Triangle
    • - This type of triangle has two equal sides while the third one is not. The third side however must be less than the sum of the two equal sides.
  • Scalene Triangle
    • - This type of triangle has no sides equal nor angles.

This application lets you know the type of triangle you are dealing with just by inputting the values of its length.

HOW IT WORKS!

You simply input 3 separate figures into each box and click submit.
The results will show if your figures form either an equilateral triangle, an isosceles triangle,a scalene triangle or if the figures do not form a triangle at all.

*Note that only numbers will be accepted. DO NOT INPUT LETTERS,PUNCTUATION MARKS OR OTHER SYMBOLS.
