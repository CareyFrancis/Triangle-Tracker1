TRIANGLE TRACKER.

This application lets you know the type of triangle you are dealing with just by inputting the values of its length.

HOW IT WORKS!

You simply input 3 separate digits into each box and click submit. The results will show if your figures form either an equilateral triangle, an isosceles triangle,a scalene triangle or if the figures do not form a triangle at all.

Please input your figures inside the following spaces. Note that only numbers will be accepted. DO NOT INPUT LETTERS,PUNCTUATION MARKS OR OTHER SYMBOLS.
